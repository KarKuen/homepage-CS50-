  • For my final year project in Singapore Polytechnic, I started my very own startup.

  • When the covid19 pandemic struck, we took the opportunity to tackle the prominent problem at hand.

  • We designed and sold disinfectant lightstrips that produced UVC rays which killed the harmful virus. The product was designed to fit in commonly shared areas such as elevators and shared cars.

  • We presented this idea to a wide mix of industrial experts during the Rocket Pitch Festival 2021 and managed to win the first place.
